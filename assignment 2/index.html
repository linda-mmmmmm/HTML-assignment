    Brand Introduction

    HTML Pizza Catering Management Co., Ltd. was established in 2010. Currently, we have over 170 stores in Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and other cities, all of which are company-owned and not franchised. We are an investment enterprise of Sequoia Capital and the first catering enterprise invested by Sequoia Capital in China. In 2011, we pioneered durian pizza, which has since become popular nationwide.
